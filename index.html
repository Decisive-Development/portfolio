Responsive Design

Websites are often accessed across multiple devices; mobiles, tablets, laptops and computers. Each device has a different screen-size, which means your website needs to look and work differently for each. A responsive website will automatically adapt images, text and interfaces to deliver the best experience to the user, through any screen.

Mobile First

Did you know, 60% of all devices connected to the internet are on mobile phones? Laptops 16%, tablets 12% and only 8% for desktop computers. This means websites are viewed more often on smaller screens than larger. A 'mobile first' development approach builds a website to scale up to desktops, rather than down to mobiles. This ensures a seamless experience for your users on any device.

SEO Implementation

SEO (Search Engine Optimisation) is one of the most important parts of a website's development. Proper SEO will boost your websites search ranking, increasing traffic to your website and thus, to your business. Lack or little optimisation can result in your website never being found, no matter how nice it is, or how long it has been running.

Bespoke UI & UX

UI (User interface) & UX (User experience) are terms used for the layout design, interactivity and quality of a website or application. It is important for your website to be appealing to view, easy to navigate and satisfying to use. This will not only keep users on your site for longer but increase the chance of them returning and sharing it with others. UI & UX fundamentals are used for your business, for your users.
